Let's taste this classic recipe in a new way.

Easy and fast to make, and just delicious. Everyone going to love this one!

To make these chicken cordon-bleus, we'll need:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Swiss cheese
  • Ham
  • Salt and Pepper

See? Not a lot of ingredients. And it don't stop there: It is really easy to prepare!

  1. Prep the Chicken!! Cover the chicken cutlets with plastic wrap and pound then with a mallet to even and thin the pieces. Then, season it thoroughly with salt and pepper.
  2. Roll! Top each piece of chicken with a slice of swiss cheese and a slice of ham. Roll the chicken vertically so that ham and cheese are contained. Secure each roll with a toothpick.
  3. Bread and bake! Arrange the chicken rolls in a prepared baking dish. Sprinkle liberally and evenly with breadcrumbs. Bake until the chicken isn't pink anymore, then top each rol with half a slice of swiss, and continue baking until the cheese melt.
