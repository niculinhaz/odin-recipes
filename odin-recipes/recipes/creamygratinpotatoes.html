A beloved recipe! Say hi to the gratined and creamy potatoes!

With a delicious look and even more delicious taste, this one is perfect for your home brunch during easter. Being so easy to make and so tasty is not for all, but this one does it with maestry. Take a note on this one, and trust me, it's worth it!

For it, you'll need:

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk

Now, let's go to the recipe itself.

  1. Slice them! First of all, slice the potatoes and the onion. Light the oven too.
  2. Prepare the casserole! After that, assemble the casserole laying half the potatoes in the bottom. Season them, and then layer the onion slices. Then, the remaining potatoes. Season again.
  3. Make the sauce! For the sauce, start melting the butter in a saucepan. Keep whisking flour and salt gradually for a minute or so, and then start whisking the milk. Don't stop whisking, and let the mixture cook until it has thickened. After that, stir in the cheese.
  4. Let's bake! Pour the sauce in the casserole. Cover the dish in foil and bake in a preheated oven until the potatoes are tender and the sauce is bubbling.

And easy as that, your delicious gratined potatoes are ready! Serve as you want, they make a great principal meal and an even better side dish. Hope you have liked it!
