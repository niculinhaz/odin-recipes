Be ready, these cinnamon rolls will be ready before you even see it.

In 90 minutes you could do a lot of things. Watch a soccer game, a movie, or make the best cinnamon rolls ever. Your choice.

For it, you'll need:

  • 3/4 cup of milk.
  • 1/4 cup of margarine
  • 3 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 package of instant yeast (.25 ounce)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup of margarine
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • (Optional) 1/2 cup raisins

Now, let's cook!

  1. Heat the milk! Heat the milk in a small saucepan until a few bubbles form around the edge. Remove from heat and stir margarine until melted.
  2. Prepare a mixture! Combine 2 1/4 cups of flour, white sugar, yeast and salt in a large bowl. Add the milk mixture, water and egg. Beat well. Add the remaining 1/4 cup of flour, half cup at a time, stirring well after each addition.
  3. Beat the dough! Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth, about 5 minutes. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Work that cinnamon! Mix the brown sugar, margarine and cinnamon in a small bowl. Set aside.
  5. Let's get hot! Preheat the oven to 375 ºF/ 190 ºC. Grease a 12-cup muffin pan.
  6. Roll the dough! Roll dough out on a lightly floured surface into a 12x9 inch rectangle. Spread the cinnamon mixture over the dough, and sprinkle with raisins.
  7. Now it's a roll! Roll up dough and pinch seam to seal. Cut into 12 equal rolls.
  8. Get that juice! Place cut-side up in the prepared muffin cups. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 30 minutes.
  9. Baking time! Bake in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until browned. Remove rolls from muffin cups to cool on a wire rack. Serve warm.

Now you have a dozen of these pretty perfect rolls! Hope you enjoyed it!
